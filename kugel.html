Kugel Recipe

History

"The first kugels were made from bread and flour and were savory rather than sweet. About 800 years ago when, Jewish cooks in Germany replaced bread mixtures with lokshen noodles or farfel. Eventually eggs were incorporated" (Wikipedia.org). From there the recipe has been adapted hundreds of times throughout many cultures. Kugel's are made with cinnamon, raisins, and other random ingrediants, for they were adapted to be that way throughout the 800 years the dish has been around.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz broad noodles
  • 8 oz package cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 stick butter, melted
  • 1 sprinkle of cinnamon

Directions

  1. Cook noodles in salted water & drain
  2. Add melted butter and mix
  3. While noodles are cooking, you can prepare the "pudding" mixture:
    1. In mixer, cream the cream cheese and sugar (mix very well)
    2. Add well beaten eggs gradually
    3. Add milk and combine well
  4. Once the noodles and mixture are complete, stir the mixture into the noodles.
  5. Pour into a 9X9 (or similar size) greased pan
  6. Sprinkle cinnamon on top
  7. Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees
